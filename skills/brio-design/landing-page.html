FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before making the switch.

How often do I need to replace the filter?
Brio filters last approximately 6 months under normal use. The cooler will remind you with a filter-life indicator light. Replacement filters are $29.99 and take under 2 minutes to swap — no tools required.
Does it require a water line?
No. Brio bottom-load and top-load coolers use standard 3 or 5-gallon water bottles. Simply place the bottle in the bottom compartment — no plumbing, no installation.
What's the return policy?
30-day no-questions-asked returns. We provide a prepaid return label and issue a full refund within 5 business days of receiving the unit.
How loud is the compressor?
Brio coolers operate at under 40 dB — quieter than a typical refrigerator. The compressor only runs when actively cooling, and sleep mode further reduces noise during off-hours.
Is the hot water child-safe?
Yes. The hot water dispenser includes a two-step child safety lock that requires pressing and sliding simultaneously. The lock can also be fully disabled if not needed.
